Vulnerability Management Services

Discover and Fortify the Weak Points in Your IT Infrastructure with Our Advanced Vulnerability Scanning Services, Tailored for Small and Medium-Sized Businesses.

What is Vulnerability Scanning?

Vulnerability Scanning is a systematic review of security weaknesses in your information systems. It's essential for proactive detection and resolution of vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

Why Vulnerability Scanning Matters

In an age where cyber threats are continuously evolving, routine vulnerability scans are not just recommended; they are a cornerstone of any robust cybersecurity strategy.

  • Key Statistics:

    Over 80% of all security incidents are due to unpatched vulnerabilities

    82% of ransomware attacks target SMBs, highlighting the urgent need for comprehensive scanning

Detailed Security Insights

Our scanning process provides comprehensive insights into your security posture, delivering actionable reports to guide your protective measures.

Regular and On-Demand Scans

We offer both scheduled and on-demand vulnerability scans to ensure that your environment remains secure against the latest known vulnerabilities.

External Vulnerability Scanning

Identify vulnerabilities on all internet-facing assets to prevent potential breaches and maintain your external security perimeter.

Internal Vulnerability Scanning

Deep dive into your internal network to detect security gaps within your infrastructure and ensure internal compliance with industry standards.

Cloud Vulnerability Scanning

Secure your cloud environments with dedicated scans that assess your cloud services and storage for potential vulnerabilities.

Statistics to Consider

Cloud exploitation grew by 95% last year, making cloud vulnerability scanning increasingly critical.

Cyberattack Impact on SMBs

  • Recovery Time and Cost: The average time for an SMB to recover from a malware attack is 7 days, with costs reaching up to $100,000+.
  • Data Breach Cost: SMBs face an average cost of $3.31 million per data breach incident.
  • Operational Impact: 58% of SMBs experience downtime due to cyberattacks, with 40% losing critical data.
